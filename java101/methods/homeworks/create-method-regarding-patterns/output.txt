Project Name: Create Method Regarding Patterns

Project Explanation:
This program uses a recursive method to subtract 5 from a number until it reaches 0 or below, printing each value.
Then as the recursion unwinds, it prints the values again in reverse (adding back 5 each time).
This demonstrates the call stack behavior of recursive methods.

Sample Output:
N sayisini giriniz: 16
16 11 6 1 -4 1 6 11 16

N sayisini giriniz: 25
25 20 15 10 5 0 5 10 15 20 25
