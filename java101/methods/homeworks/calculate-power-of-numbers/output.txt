Project Name: Calculate Power Of Numbers

Project Explanation:
This program takes a base and exponent from the user and calculates the power using a recursive method.
The recursive method multiplies the base by itself exp times, returning 1 when the exponent reaches 0.

Sample Output:
Taban degerini giriniz: 2
Us degerini giriniz: 3
Sonuc: 8

Taban degerini giriniz: 5
Us degerini giriniz: 3
Sonuc: 125
