Project Name: Calculate Salary

Project Explanation:
This project defines an Employee class that calculates tax, bonus, and salary raise for factory workers.
Tax is 3% if salary exceeds 1000 TL; bonus is 30 TL per extra hour over 40 hours per week.
Raise is 5%, 10%, or 15% based on years of service (less than 10, less than 20, or 20+ years).

Sample Output:
Adi: kemal
Maasi: 2000.0
Calisma Saati: 45
Baslangic Yili: 1985
Vergi: 60.0
Bonus: 150.0
Maas Artisi: 300.0
Vergi ve Bonuslar ile birlikte maas: 2090.0
