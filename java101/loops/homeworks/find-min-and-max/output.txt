Project Name: Find Min and Max

Project Explanation:
This program takes N numbers from the user and finds the largest and smallest among them using a loop.
It iterates through each entered number, tracking the current minimum and maximum values, then prints both at the end.

Sample Output:
Kac tane sayi gireceksiniz: 4
1. Sayiyi giriniz: 16
2. Sayiyi giriniz: -22
3. Sayiyi giriniz: -15
4. Sayiyi giriniz: 100
En buyuk sayi: 100
En kucuk sayi: -22
