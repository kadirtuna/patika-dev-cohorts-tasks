Project Name: Make Triangle With Stars

Project Explanation:
This program prints a triangle and a diamond shape made of stars using nested loops.
The triangle is built by increasing the star count per row with leading spaces for alignment.
The diamond extends the triangle by mirroring it bottom-up for the second half.

Sample Output (n=4):
Ucgen:

   ***
  *****
 *******
*********
