Project Name: Find Harmonic Numbers

Project Explanation:
This program takes a number n from the user and calculates the harmonic series sum up to n.
The harmonic series is the sum of 1/1 + 1/2 + 1/3 + ... + 1/n.
It uses a loop to accumulate each term and prints the final result.

Sample Output:
Sayi giriniz: 5
5. harmonik seri toplami: 2.283333333333333

Sayi giriniz: 10
10. harmonik seri toplami: 2.9289682539682538
