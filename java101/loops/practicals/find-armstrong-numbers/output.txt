Project Name: Find Armstrong Numbers

Project Explanation:
This program checks whether a given number is an Armstrong number.
An Armstrong number is one where the sum of each digit raised to the power of the total digit count equals the number itself.
As a bonus, it also calculates and prints the sum of the digits of the entered number.

Sample Output:
Sayi giriniz: 407
407 bir Armstrong sayisidir.
407 sayisinin basamak toplami: 11

Sayi giriniz: 1342
1342 bir Armstrong sayisi degildir.
1342 sayisinin basamak toplami: 10
