Project Name: Print Letter With Arrays

Project Explanation:
This program prints the letter B using stars (*) and a 2D string array.
Rows at index 0, 3, and 6 are fully filled with stars to form the horizontal bars of B.
The first and last columns of other rows form the vertical sides.

Sample Output:
 *  *  *  *
 *        *
 *        *
 *  *  *  *
 *        *
 *        *
 *  *  *  *
